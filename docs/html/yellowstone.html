Yellowstone National Park

A Photgraphic Exploration, 2019

In the summer of 2019 we went on a family camping trip to Yellowstone National Park. This was my first time at the park so I knew I would be in awe by all the beauty surrounding me. I wanted to explore all the different landscapes within Yellowstone- both big and small. Of course, everyone knows and loves Old Faithful, and it is indeed an amazing sight to behold. However, I also wanted to spend time looking through the camera lens at the views within Yellowstone that are not always noticed. All rights reserved.
