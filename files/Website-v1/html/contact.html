I Am Anna of The Trees

I have been an artist my whole life. When I am creating art in my free time I usually work in the mediums of photography, printmaking, or pen and ink. There is something special that happens when you work with your hands and create something. Though, I realised along the way as an artist that there is that same feeling when working in the graphic arts and in web design.

Web design is a form of creating something that can easily be shared. It was something I fell in love with as a kid, but took a step away from for a while. I am back to it now and love the process of tinkering with the code, graphics, and user experience.

Outside of web design and art, I love to bake, do a little yoga in the sunshine, and spend time with my family. I live near the mountains, and that's where my soul belongs. Any time I can get away you will find me in the mountains hiking or climbing around on the rocks- or as my husband and I like to joke, billy-goating around.

I am always open to new opportunities and ideas. If you would like to create something with me or simply have a question, please reach out. I would love to chat.

Come follow me on Instagram and my other sites for more updates!
